# At the Lake

Memory lane,
A place of deep reprieve
And rampant pain.

What went wrong?
Where did we go?
We grasp and plead
Though cannot know.

We wish for anger
So we may find
A freeing pain,
A friend of kind.

Our minds are racing
Yet quiet we are.
We feel so close,
And yet so far.

* Kathryn Exline
(October 2016)
